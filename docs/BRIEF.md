# 2. Business Intelligence Plots

**Difficulty Level:** Easy

## Overview

In this guided Tableau project, you will step into the role of a Business Analyst at **Adventure Works**, tasked with analysing sales data to provide valuable insights for decision-making.

Using Tableau's powerful visualisation tools, you will:

- Compare online and offline sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products across different sales channels.
- Build interactive dashboards to communicate findings effectively.

By applying core Tableau skills such as calculated fields, filters, interactive dashboards, and dual-axis visualisations, you will create a comprehensive business intelligence solution that demonstrates your ability to derive actionable insights from complex retail sales data.

This hands-on project strengthens Tableau proficiency while providing practical experience in creating executive-level dashboards that support strategic decision-making in a real-world retail environment.

## Prerequisites

To successfully complete this project, you should be comfortable with the following Tableau fundamentals:

- Navigating the Tableau interface and distinguishing between dimensions and measures.
- Constructing foundational chart types including bar charts, line charts, and scatter plots.
- Developing and interpreting calculated fields to enhance analysis.
- Applying filters to improve dashboard interactivity.
- Creating basic dashboards that combine multiple visualisations.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Load and Explore the Adventure Works Sales Dataset

- Import the Adventure Works Excel dataset into Tableau.
- Create relationships between sales, customer, product, and geography tables.
- Validate relationship cardinality and referential integrity.

---

### 2. Create Visualisations to Compare Online vs Offline Orders

- Create calculated fields to identify sales channels.
- Build comparative visualisations showing online and offline sales performance.
- Analyse revenue distribution across sales channels.

---

### 3. Develop Scatter Plots to Analyse Product Performance Across Channels

- Create scatter plots comparing:
  - Units Sold
  - Revenue
  - Product Categories
  - Sales Channels
- Use colour and size encodings to highlight trends and outliers.

---

### 4. Design Interactive Tooltips with Embedded Visualisations

- Implement Viz in Tooltip functionality.
- Create embedded revenue comparisons directly within product tooltips.
- Enable users to explore additional context without leaving the main visualisation.

---

### 5. Build a Comprehensive Dashboard Combining Key Visualisations

Create an executive dashboard containing:

- KPI Cards
  - Total Revenue
  - Units Sold
  - Orders
  - Revenue per Unit

- Online vs Offline Revenue Comparison
- Product Performance Scatter Plot
- Interactive Filters
- Dynamic Tooltips

---

### 6. Summarise Findings and Identify Actionable Next Steps

Analyse the dashboard outputs to identify:

- Sales channel performance trends.
- High-performing products and categories.
- Opportunities for online sales growth.
- Cross-selling opportunities.
- Product portfolio optimisation opportunities.

Develop actionable business recommendations based on the findings to support strategic decision-making at Adventure Works.
